How I Work

First Principles.
Always.

I don't believe in guesswork. Every solution I build is grounded in evidence, shaped by constraints, and tested against reality.

  • 01
    Understand the Core Problem
    Start with first principles. What is actually broken and for whom? I resist jumping to solutions before the problem is fully mapped.
  • 02
    Collect Evidence
    User interviews, market research, competitor analysis, and data. I talk to real people before writing a single requirement.
  • 03
    Work Out a Solution Within Constraints
    Good solutions aren't perfect — they're right for the time, team, and resources available. I design with trade-offs in mind.
  • 04
    Align Stakeholders
    Build-in-silence fails. I bring in legal, ops, engineering, and leadership early to shape and de-risk the path forward.
  • 05
    Build & Ship
    Move fast with quality. Whether vibe-coding a CRM or shipping an IoT product, I bias toward doing over debating.
  • 06
    Learn, Iterate & Scale
    Post-launch data, NPS, and funnel metrics tell the real story. I close the loop and push for continuous improvement.
